The handover

It only counts when your team can run it without me.

No black box, no dependency on me. Your nominated owner has to pass a real, three-cycle handover before the job is done.

1

I run it, narrating

First cycle is mine — your owner watches every step and why.

2

They run it, I’m on the gates

Your owner drives; I only step in at the checkpoints.

3

They run it solo

Owner runs it alone. It’s only complete if this cycle passes the acceptance checklist.

That handover needs one capable person on your side to take it on — the “AI owner”. It’s the same designated-learner every business had when I connected them to the internet in the 90s.
